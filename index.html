KUMARAGURU INSTITUTION'S

a multifaceted hub of learning instituted in 1984 with Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), KCT Business School (KCT BS) in 2005, Kumaraguru Institute of Agriculture (KIA) in 2014 Kumaraguru College of Liberal Arts and Science (KCLAS) in 2018 and Kumaraguru School of Business (KSB) in 2022, is an integral part of Sakthi Group established by visionary statesman Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam.

Commemorating 41 years of educational excellence, Kumaraguru Institutions remains committed to its founding vision of empowering conscientious young minds through transformative learning experience.

About

The Kumaraguru Business Network (KBN) proudly represents the legacy, growth, and vibrant community of the esteemed Kumaraguru Institutions. Rooted in a rich tradition of academic excellence and holistic development, Kumaraguru Institutions have flourished over the years, nurturing generations of leaders, innovators, and change-makers. KBN connects this vast network of alumni and professionals, fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and business growth. United by shared values and a commitment to continuous learning, KBN empowers its members to contribute meaningfully to society while strengthening the global Kumaraguru community.

Vision

We envision cultivating a culture of continuous learning, collaboration, and forward-thinking. Our aim is to be a catalyst for positive change, nurturing influential leaders who shape industries and contribute meaningfully to societal progress.

Mission

Our mission at KBN is to cultivate a thriving global community of Kumaraguru alumni by fostering meaningful connections, sharing knowledge, and creating opportunities for continuous learning and growth.
